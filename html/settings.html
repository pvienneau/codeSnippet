You can easily let your clients pay you with PayPal by enabling the PayPal integration. A PayPal payment link will be generated and placed on the client's invoice page. The Invoice Machine will be automatically notified and will add the payment to the history and change the status to Paid when an invoice is fully paid. Your client doesn't need a PayPal account to pay you, just a credit card. If you don't have a PayPal account you can signup for one free.

Send a notification by email when a payment is made.

By enabling the Basecamp integration you can access your Basecamp projects, time entries, companies and people directly in The Invoice Machine. When you create or edit an invoice, a "Insert From Basecamp" option will be added into the insert menu and when you create or edit a client, a "Import from Basecamp" button will be available. In order for the integration to work, you need to enable the Basecamp API in your Basecamp account and enter your URL, username and password below. We never share this information with anyone.
